Crescent City, CA

If you don't mind some fog and cooler temperatures, Crescent City is a small coastal town in northern California. It has easy access to both beaches (though cold) and multiple redwood parks.

Things to do:

  • Jedediah Smith Redwoods State Park
  • Stout Grove
  • Battery Point Lighthouse
  • Crescent Beach
  • Crescent City Harbor

Places to stay:

  • Ocean View Inn
  • Best Western Plus Northwoods Inn
  • Crescent Beach Motel
  • The Lighthouse Inn
